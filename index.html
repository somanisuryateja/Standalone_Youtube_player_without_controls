Clean YouTube Player

Branding-free playback — no YouTube logo, title, share, or recommendations.

How it works: the embed uses controls=0, a custom poster that covers YouTube's start/pause screens, and an iframe crop that hides the title bar and watermark during playback. All playback runs through your own control bar.

Not DRM: the video is still served by YouTube's free CDN and the source ID is discoverable via devtools. For paid/secure content use Cloudflare Stream or Vimeo Pro with signed URLs.
